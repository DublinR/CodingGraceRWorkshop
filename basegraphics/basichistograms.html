To create a histogram of the car weights from the Cars93 data set \footnotesize \begin{framed} \begin{verbatim} hist(mtcars$mpg, main="Histogram of MPG (Data: MTCARS) ") \end{verbatim} \end{framed} \texttt{R} automatically chooses the number and width of the bars. We can change this by specifying the location of the break points. \begin{framed} \begin{verbatim} hist(Cars93$Weight, breaks=c(1500, 2050, 2300, 2350, 2400, 2500, 3000, 3500, 3570, 4000, 4500), xlab="Weight", main="Histogram of Weight") \end{verbatim} \end{framed} \end{document}
